Financing Information

Thanks for selecting to pay via finance. To complete this you will be directed to the checkout to pay the deposit and any non-finance products in your order.

Once you have completed this payment, you will be redirected to the Hitachi Capital Website to fill out your online credit application. This should not take more than 5 minutes and you will get an instant decision.

IMPORTANT: You will need print, sign and send the agreement provided by Hitachi Capital back to them before we can ship the products. Please do this as soon as possible after having the online application accepted to ensure we ship to you as soon as possible.

Finance Criteria

Before you continue, please ensure that you meet the following criteria:

  • You are able to provide a residential address not a business address
  • You are a resident at this address
  • You are a UK resident for over 12 months and intend to stay in the UK
  • You are 18 or older
  • You or your partner is, in permanent paid employment (over 16 hours per week), retired and receiving a pension or are self employed
  • You are able to provide acceptable proof of address (if delivery address is different to that used in the credit agreement) and proof of signature
  • You are purchasing the product for consumer use and not business use

If you fail to meet these criteria, please contact us
